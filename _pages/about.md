Welcome to my page! I am a twenty-eight-year-old American living in Vancouver. I currently work as an Operations Systems Specialist at [FISPAN](https://fispan.com), a financial services company that has partnered with 15 of the top U.S. banks - including J.P. Morgan Chase - with combined assets exceeding $3T. I design, build, and maintain secure, scalable automation systems that support customer and revenue operations in a regulated fintech environment. My work focuses on the administration and configuration of the core systems ued by the Operations department, analyze and design business processes, build and maintain their automation architecture, and ensure data integrity and reliability across interconnected platforms.

Up until now, my academic and professional life has exposed me to a wide range of industries and technologies, and, as a result, I have been able to develop a relatively diverse skill set. These skills span from business process automation and workflow creation, to software administration, to project management, to content creation, to proper utilization of AI tools for multimodal and agentic workflows, and more. I specialize in low/no-code automation platforms like Make.com and n8n, where I design production-grade workflows that orchestrate data across multiple systems with an emphasis on security, reliability, and maintainability. Most recently, I completed a company-wide migration from Make to n8n in approximately six weeks, rebuilding nearly every automation while improving long-term scalability.

In addition, I spend much of my free time learning to program. In the beginning of 2024, I took part in [Harvard's CS50: Introduction to Computer Science](https://pll.harvard.edu/course/cs50-introduction-computer-science) course where I was prompted to solve problems in a variety of languages, including C, Python, SQL, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript. I took the principles I learned from the course, and applied them in my own work, some of which can be found in the [Projects](https://ddugan23.github.io/projects/) tab.

My academic background includes a B.A. in Economics and an A.A. in Film Production, which together shaped my structured, systems-oriented approach to operational work.

Outside of work, I spend my time running, hiking, reading, playing chess, and learning new things. I'm currently learning Go (Weiqi, Baduk) and Mandarin!
